Hello [data-driven] world

I am Guillermo Villar, a data journalist interested in the use of programming at the service of information sciences.

I hold a Master's degree in Data Science from The Netherlands to improve my skills in data journalism through R and Python. I enjoy with the application of data analysis, text mining and data visualization (ggplot2 and online tools). Before this, I did a Master's degree (título propio) in data journalism and I also graduated in Journalism with Extraordinary Prize (best student award) and in Audiovisual Communication.

My portfolio contains a selection of the projects from my data-journalism-related internships at two of the most important newsrooms in Spain (El País and RTVE -Lab RTVE-) and from my collaboration through data-oriented content at a communication agency focused on digital transformation. If you are interested in all what I can do nowadays, send me a message.
